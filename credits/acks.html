Turns out that it takes a whole lot of people and a whole lot of work to make a project and web site like this function. Add to that the difficulty of keeping content current when the project is changing daily, and you have a need for a great supporting cast. We wouldn't be here without the help of the following people and groups:

James Duncan Davidson
James was instrumental in helping us get this project off the ground. He continually serves as an advisor to the project, keeping us on the road to Java and XML usability.

Pier Fumagalli
Pier, a giant over in Apache-land, experimented with James on another project with a similar purpose, and some of his ideas made it over into the JDOMTM project. Thanks to Pier for good design and willingness to let us run with this thing.
wantJAVA and James Ward
James W., as we know him, offered to host this site for free using services provided from wantJava, a servlet- and JSP-hosting ISP. He has been extremely helpful; if you are in need of servlet hosting, check them out!
